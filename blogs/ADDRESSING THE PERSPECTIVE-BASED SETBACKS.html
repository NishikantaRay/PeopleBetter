ADDRESSING THE PERSPECTIVE-BASED SETBACKS

It is largely seen that the management of human resources is undervalued. Studies say, more than 93% have limited or zero knowledge about the importance of people. Approximately 20% of businesses are unsure how human capital fits into their operations. 58% of business owners feel that the HR functions do not need information on the company’s financial statistics. 71% of MSME Business owners opine that HR functions are more about employees’ interests and less inclined towards the company’s Goal. 15% of the respondents, HR has two important roles: resolving employee grievances and handling benefits. In the opinion of 54% of the respondents, staff training is second to other requirements. 67% of the respondents feel that HR is only responsible for recruiting employees. 34% of business owners believe HR’s primary role is to increase employee efficiency. These mindsets prove to be a setback for the organizations.

It’s crucial for MSMEs to recognize the value of people as a critical resource and to invest in effective HR practices to attract, retain, and develop talent.

People are the driving force behind business success. Without a strong focus on human capital, businesses may struggle to attract top talent and retain employees who are essential to the company's success.

HR solutions like training and development programs, performance management systems, and employee engagement initiatives can help MSMEs boost productivity, reduce turnover, and improve overall performance.

In today's competitive job market, MSMEs that prioritize employee well-being and create a positive workplace culture can attract and retain top talent, giving them a competitive advantage.

MSMEs that do not have sound HR practices in place may be at risk for legal and financial penalties due to noncompliance with employment laws, wrongful termination, or discrimination lawsuits. Good HR practices can reduce these legal and financial risks.

In summary, MSMEs should recognize that their employees are a valuable asset and prioritize human capital and HR solutions to ensure that they are attracting and retaining top talent, improving productivity and efficiency, reducing legal and financial risks, and creating a positive workplace culture.
